# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We want this project to be welcoming, practical, and respectful.

Examples of positive behavior:
- being respectful and constructive
- assuming good intent while giving clear feedback
- helping beginners and non-developers participate safely
- sharing context instead of sarcasm

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- sharing private data, secrets, or credentials in public spaces
- abusive, threatening, or intentionally disruptive conduct
- spam or bad-faith contributions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or contributions that violate these standards.
Repeated or serious violations may lead to restricted participation.

## Reporting

If conduct in this repository becomes abusive or unsafe, contact the maintainers privately rather than escalating in a public thread.
